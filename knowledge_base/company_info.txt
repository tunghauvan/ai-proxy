# Company Information - Acme Corporation

## About Us
Acme Corporation is a leading technology company founded in 2010. We specialize in building innovative AI-powered solutions for enterprise customers. Our headquarters is located in San Francisco, California, with offices in New York, London, and Tokyo.

## Mission Statement
Our mission is to democratize artificial intelligence and make it accessible to businesses of all sizes. We believe that AI should be a tool for empowerment, not replacement.

## Our Products

### AcmeAI Assistant
Our flagship product, AcmeAI Assistant, is an enterprise-grade conversational AI platform. Key features include:
- Natural language understanding
- Multi-language support (50+ languages)
- Custom knowledge base integration
- Role-based access control
- SOC 2 Type II certified

### AcmeRAG Platform
AcmeRAG Platform enables businesses to build retrieval-augmented generation systems. It supports:
- Multiple document formats (PDF, Word, Excel, HTML)
- Vector database integration
- Semantic search capabilities
- Real-time document updates

### AcmeAnalytics
AcmeAnalytics provides AI-powered business intelligence:
- Predictive analytics
- Anomaly detection
- Custom dashboards
- Automated reporting
